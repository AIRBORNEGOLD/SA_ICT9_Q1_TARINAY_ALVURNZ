Mayon Bicol

Mayon Volcano is an active volcano in the province of Albay in Bicol Region, on the island of Luzon in the Philippines. Famous for its perfect cone shape, it is a must-visit destination for nature lovers.

Boracay

Boracay is a tropical paradise known for its white sandy beaches and crystal-clear waters. It is a popular destination for beach lovers, water sports enthusiasts, and those who want to experience nightlife.

Banaue Rice Terraces

The Banaue Rice Terraces are ancient terraces carved into the mountains of Ifugao in the Philippines. Often referred to as the "Eighth Wonder of the World," these terraces showcase the ingenuity of the Ifugao people.
